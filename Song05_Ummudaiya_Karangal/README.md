Psalm 119:73-80 (Yod) – “Your hands have made me and fashioned me.” The psalmist thanks God for creating him and asks for understanding to learn His commandments. Even in affliction, he finds comfort in God’s Word.
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, Your hands formed and fashioned me.
Give me understanding to learn Your law and a thankful heart that never forgets Your mercy.
