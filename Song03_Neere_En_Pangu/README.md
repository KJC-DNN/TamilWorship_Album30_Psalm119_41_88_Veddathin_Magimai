# நீரே என் பங்கு | You Are My Portion | Album 30 Song 3

ஆல்பம் 30 பாடல் 3
நீரே என் பங்கு – சங்கீதம் 119:57-64 (சேத்).
இரவில் எழுந்து துதித்தல். அல்லேலூயா!

Psalm 119:57-64 (Cheth) – “You are my portion, O Lord.” The psalmist declares God as his inheritance, rises at midnight to thank Him, and chooses obedience even when the wicked surround him.

Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus, You are my portion and my inheritance.
Help me rise at night to praise You and obey Your Word.
In darkness and trial, let Your mercy revive me. 
Amen. Hallelujah!
